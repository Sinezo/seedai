Who are we?


Amakhuthala Electrical & Electronic Design and Installation (AEEDAI) is an electrical and electronic business initiative whose focus is on the design and installation of electrical and electronic systems.

We employ laws and principles of Electrical Engineering to invent and improve devices and systems out there. Through our thorough planning and necessary experiments, we produce the best quality and satisfactory products and services.



Why us?


We value you. In the process of designing we have your safety on our minds. A system without protection is like a ticking bomb. We make sure even in case of faults, you will never be affected. We achieve this by employing the modern electrical protection and automation.
